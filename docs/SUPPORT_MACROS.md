# Support Macros

Korean translation: `docs/ko/SUPPORT_MACROS.md`

## Login

We will check your phone verification status first. Please confirm the phone
number, country code, device model, OS version, and the time of the failed
attempt.

## STT Failure

Voice messages can be sent after retrying STT or manually editing the transcript.
Please share the approximate recording time, language, device, and whether the
audio was sent in review mode or instant mode.

## Lost Account

Accounts are tied to phone authentication and user ID. Please provide the phone
number country code, user ID, and the last successful login date.

## Report Handling

Reports are reviewed with the minimum necessary message or room context. We may
take action on messages, rooms, or accounts depending on severity.

## Data Deletion

Use Menu -> Account management -> account deletion to remove profile data,
handle reservation, push tokens, and active room membership. Existing sent
messages are anonymized for conversation continuity unless individually deleted
before account deletion.
