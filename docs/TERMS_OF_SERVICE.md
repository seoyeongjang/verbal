# Terms of Service Draft

Korean translation: `docs/ko/TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md`

## Acceptable Use

Users may not use Verbal for spam, harassment, illegal content,
impersonation, malware, or attempts to disrupt the service.

## Messaging

Users are responsible for messages, voice audio, transcripts, attachments, and
locations they send. Sent messages may be retained for recipients unless the
sender deletes the message or account deletion policy applies.

## Voice Transcription

STT is provided to improve messaging usability. Transcripts may be inaccurate;
voice messages are sent automatically after STT succeeds, and users can retry
STT or manually enter a transcript when STT fails.

## Safety

The service may review reports, restrict invites, remove content, disable rooms,
or suspend accounts to protect users and the platform.

## Availability

During beta, features may change and the service may have interruptions.

## Contact

Set the production legal/support contact before public launch.
