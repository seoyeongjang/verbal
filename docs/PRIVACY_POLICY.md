# Privacy Policy Draft

Korean translation: `docs/ko/PRIVACY_POLICY.md`

Public URL: `https://verbal.chat/privacy`

Verbal collects the minimum data needed to provide authentication,
messaging, voice transcription, safety, notifications, and service operations.

## Data We Process

- Phone authentication identifier.
- User ID/handle and display name.
- Messages, voice audio, transcripts, attachments, and shared locations.
- Room membership, invite links, reports, blocks, and safety metadata.
- Push notification token.
- Usage diagnostics for cost, reliability, and abuse monitoring.

## Voice and STT

Voice messages are transcribed by STT. In production, Deepgram may receive audio
for transcription. Audio retention is room-based and defaults to 1 day; after
expiry, audio is deleted and transcript text remains.

## User Controls

- Delete sent messages.
- Change room audio retention settings where permitted.
- Export account data from `Data and storage`.
- Delete account from `Menu -> Account management`.
- Report rooms or messages.
- Block users.

## Sharing

Data is shared with infrastructure providers only as needed to operate the app,
including Firebase/Google Cloud and Deepgram for STT.

## Contact

Privacy and data deletion questions can be sent to `support@verbal.chat`.
