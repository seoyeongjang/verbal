# Community Guidelines Draft

Korean translation: `docs/ko/COMMUNITY_GUIDELINES.md`

Status date: 2026-06-18

Verbal is a messaging service where users can send text, voice, transcripts,
attachments, locations, calendar proposals, and open-chat invitations. These
guidelines apply to user-generated content and behavior in Verbal.

## Not Allowed

- Spam, scam, phishing, malware, or deceptive links.
- Harassment, threats, hate, sexual exploitation, or illegal content.
- Impersonation or attempts to mislead users about identity or affiliation.
- Sharing another person's private information without permission.
- Abuse of invite links, open chats, reports, or automation.
- Attempts to bypass safety systems, rate limits, or retention controls.

## User Controls

- Report messages or rooms.
- Block users.
- Leave rooms.
- Delete sent messages where supported.
- Revoke invite links where permitted.

## Enforcement

Verbal may remove content, limit invites, restrict open chats, suspend accounts,
or preserve safety records when needed to protect users or operate the service.
Reports are reviewed using available message, room, account, and safety
metadata.

## Contact

Safety and policy questions can be sent to `support@verbal.chat`.
